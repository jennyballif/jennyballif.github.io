About

Four years ago I volunteered to do science experiments for my child’s first grade class. The kids started calling me “Science Mom” and the name stuck and the gig grew; within a year I was visiting ten classrooms a week. Now I have a YouTube channel and make foldable booklets (Science Mom Guides).

The world needs more curiosity, wonder, and investigative thinking—and that’s just what these Science Mom Guides aim to encourage. So share them with your kids, parents, classroom, teachers, and friends. All of them are free and may be copied and distributed for home or educational use.
