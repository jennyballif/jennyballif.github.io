Join us!

Each year Science Mom visits thousands of elemenary school students in Southern Nevada, and her videos reach many more around the world. From the beginning, this outreach has been supported by wonderful friends and science enthusiasts.

You are cordially invited to join us on www.patreon.com/ScienceMom. On Patreon you can pledge monthly support (as little as $3 per month) and receive access to an electronic library of educational worksheets, doodle notes, and ebooks. If you would like to make a one-time donation to support our educational outreach or sponsor a school visit, you can do so at https://www.paypal.me/ScienceMom.
