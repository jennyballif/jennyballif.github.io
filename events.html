Sign up for a Meet and Greet before the Earth Science Course.

We hope you’ll join us for one of our small group meetups! This is a chance for you to get to know a few of your classmates and chat with us face to face. Each meetup will be limited to 12 participants. We’re going to share two of our favorite books we’ve read recently, and we’d love to have you to bring something to share too! It can be a favorite book or hobby, your family pet, or a question for us.

Zoom vs Google Meet

The time slots on Mondays and Fridays use Google Meet, and the time slots on Wednesdays and Saturdays use Zoom. Please choose a time slot with the platform you are most comfortable using to minimize technical difficulties or trouble-shooting. If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to email us at jenny@science.mom. We’re so excited for our Earth Science class!
