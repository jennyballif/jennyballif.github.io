Science Mom

Engaging Science lessons and activities for kids of all ages -

  • In 2014, I volunteered to bring weekly science demonstrations to my sons's class. My name changed from "Andrew's Mom" to "Science Mom" and it stuck. I created a YouTube channel, and I visit over 10,000 students each year in Clark County Nevada.
  • We learn better with hands-on and experiential science activities. That's exactly what my doodle notes and videos aim to bring.
